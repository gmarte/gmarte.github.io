# Executive Website Design Guidelines

When working on this project (generating code, designing UI/UX, or writing copy), you must strictly adhere to the following core guidelines:

- **Executive Positioning**: Prioritize executive credibility over flashy gimmicks. The goal is to position Giancarlo as a high-impact technology leader, not just a developer.
- **Professional Aesthetics**: Do not make this look like a freelance developer portfolio. Ensure the website feels premium, innovative, modern, and memorable.
- **Beyond Coding**: Do not center the website around coding alone. Focus on enterprise scale, operational complexity, business impact, and strategic technology thinking.
- **Branding Restraint**: Do not overuse SAP branding. Balance SAP expertise with broader enterprise systems leadership and AI-driven innovation.
- **Mature Design**: Do not use childish 3D or gaming-style effects. Any 3D or motion (e.g., Three.js) must be meaningful, refined, high-performance, and elegant.
- **Unique Identity**: Do not make the visual identity look like a standard corporate or generic template. Ensure a high-end enterprise innovation feel with disciplined design choices.
